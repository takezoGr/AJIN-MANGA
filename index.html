About the Manga

Ajin: Demi-Human is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gamon Sakurai. It follows Kei Nagai, a student who discovers he is an immortal "Ajin" after surviving a fatal accident. Hunted by the government, he must survive in a world that fears and exploits his kind.
